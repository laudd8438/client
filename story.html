Our Story

What started out as a hobby for Steven Kraus quickly became an obsession, and more importantly a small business. Steven started out at a smaller location and he was the only employee. Today, he is in a bigger store with 5 employees. Kraus is now co-owner with his cousin Roscoe Rehl. Kraus says his favorite part of owning Loon's Eye is to be able to share his love for owning physical music and having a huge variety for all to enjoy looking through.

Bring in your old music! We buy used CDs, vinyl, and audio equipment.
